PromptShield

Automatically detect and replace sensitive information in your text with secure placeholders

Features

20+ Entity Types

Detects emails, phones, credit cards, names, locations, JWT tokens, and more

Multi-language

Automatically detects language and translates placeholders to match

NLP-Powered

Uses advanced NLP for accurate name and location detection

How It Works

1

Detect

Scans your text for sensitive information using regex patterns and NLP

2

Replace

Replaces detected entities with consistent placeholders like [EMAIL_1], [NAME_1]

3

Translate

Automatically translates placeholders to match your text's language

4

Protect

Your sensitive data is now safely anonymized and ready to use
